Privacy Policy  ·  Last updated: April 2026

Your privacy matters to us. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding your data. Redline does not sell your personal data to third parties — ever.

1. Who We Are

Redline ("we," "us," "our") is a mobile application designed for car enthusiasts to track drives, share locations with trusted crew members, and communicate in real time. This Privacy Policy applies to all users of the Redline iOS application.

For privacy-related questions, contact us at: redlineapp.privacy@gmail.com

2. Information We Collect

We collect the following categories of information when you use Redline:

Category What We Collect Why
Account Info Full name, email address, phone number, username, password (hashed) To create and manage your account
Vehicle Info Year, make, model, color of your car To display on your profile and crew features
Location Data Precise GPS coordinates, speed, heading, altitude — continuously while app is active Live crew tracking, drive logging, meetup features
Drive History Route coordinates, top speed, average speed, distance, duration, timestamps Drive history, stats, and badge system
Contacts Phone numbers and emails from your device contacts To find friends already on Redline and invite others
Audio Voice audio transmitted through the push-to-talk radio feature Real-time crew communication
Device Info Device type, OS version, push notification token Push notifications, app compatibility
Usage Data App features used, session duration, crash reports App improvement and bug fixing

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect solely to operate and improve the Redline app. Specifically:

  • Location data is used to display your position to crew members you have approved, log your drive routes, and enable meetup destination features
  • Drive data is used to build your personal drive history, calculate stats, and award achievement badges
  • Contact data is used only to match your contacts against existing Redline users so you can connect with friends. We do not store your full contact list — only the matched results are retained
  • Audio data is transmitted in real time through our audio provider (Agora) and is not recorded or stored by Redline
  • Push notification tokens are used exclusively to send you notifications about crew activity, invites, and app updates
  • Usage data is used to improve app performance and fix bugs

4. Information Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third parties. We share your information only in the following limited circumstances:

  • With crew members you approve — your name, car details, location, and speed are visible to members of crews you have joined. You control this by managing your crew memberships.
  • With service providers — we use third-party services to operate the app. Each provider only receives the data necessary to perform their function:
Provider Purpose Data Shared
Google Firebase Authentication, database, cloud functions Account info, location, drive data
Google Maps Map display and location search Location coordinates
Agora Real-time audio (walkie talkie) Audio stream, user ID

Apple iOS platform services As required by iOS
  • For legal compliance — we may disclose information if required by law, court order, or government authority
  • To protect safety — we may share information if we believe in good faith that disclosure is necessary to prevent harm or illegal activity

5. Location Data

Redline requests "Always On" location permission to enable background tracking while you drive. This means the app can access your location even when it is not open on screen.

We use this permission exclusively for:

  • Sharing your live location with approved crew members
  • Auto-detecting when a drive has started and logging the route
  • Detecting arrival at meetup destinations

Your location is never shared with anyone outside your approved crews. You can revoke location permission at any time in your iPhone Settings → Privacy & Security → Location Services → Redline.

6. Microphone Access

We request microphone access exclusively for the push-to-talk radio feature. Audio is only transmitted when you actively hold the talk button or enable open mic mode. Audio is streamed in real time through Agora's encrypted infrastructure and is not recorded or stored by Redline.

You can revoke microphone permission at any time in your iPhone Settings → Privacy & Security → Microphone → Redline.

7. Contacts Access

We request access to your contacts to help you find friends already on Redline. When you grant contacts permission:

  • We read phone numbers and emails from your contacts
  • We match them against Redline user accounts
  • We show you which contacts are already on the app
  • We do not upload or store your full contact list on our servers
  • Contact matching happens in batches and only matched results are retained

8. Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as your account is active. Specifically:

  • Account data — retained until you delete your account
  • Drive history — retained until you delete individual drives or your account
  • Location data — live location is not permanently stored. Drive route coordinates are stored as part of your drive history
  • Audio — not stored at all. Real-time only.

When you delete your account, we permanently delete all your personal data from our systems within 30 days.

9. Data Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your information including:

  • All data in transit is encrypted using HTTPS/TLS
  • Firebase security rules restrict access so users can only read data they are authorized to see
  • Passwords are hashed and never stored in plain text
  • Agora audio streams are encrypted end-to-end
  • API keys are stored server-side and not exposed in the app

No system is completely secure. If you discover a security vulnerability, please contact us immediately at redlineapp.privacy@gmail.com.

10. Children's Privacy

Redline is not intended for users under 16 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 16. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us at redlineapp.privacy@gmail.com and we will delete that information promptly.

11. Your Rights & Choices

You have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • Access — you can view your account information and drive history within the app at any time
  • Correction — you can update your profile information in the Settings screen
  • Deletion — you can delete your account and all associated data in Settings → Delete Account. This is permanent and cannot be undone.
  • Location control — you can revoke location access in iPhone Settings at any time
  • Notification control — you can manage notification preferences in app Settings or iPhone Settings
  • Contacts control — you can revoke contacts access in iPhone Settings at any time

12. California Privacy Rights (CCPA)

If you are a California resident, you have additional rights under the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA):

  • The right to know what personal information we collect and how it is used
  • The right to delete your personal information
  • The right to opt out of the sale of your personal information — we do not sell your personal information
  • The right to non-discrimination for exercising your privacy rights

To exercise your California privacy rights, contact us at redlineapp.privacy@gmail.com.

13. International Users

Redline is operated in the United States. If you access the App from outside the United States, your information may be transferred to and processed in the United States, where data protection laws may differ from those in your country. By using the App, you consent to this transfer.
